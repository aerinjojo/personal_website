Fab-Fod Zine

Film, Fashion, Fanfare, Fodder, published on Fridays

This zine was started as a mini arts-and-crafts project with my good friend Vicky Liao where we shared recent movies watched and music listened to. After sharing a copy with my creative writing professor, she mentioned that the department was looking for student projects to fund and graciously decided to champion the Fab-Fod zine. We started weekly editions, printing, cutting, gluing, and distributing zines to other students and professors. It was a wonderful experience and an exploration of creative liberties and expression.
